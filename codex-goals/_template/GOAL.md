# Goal

## Objective

State one coherent PM-owned objective.

- Out of scope:

## Done When

- Required work is implemented, blocked, or explicitly ruled out.
- Required direct checks are run, or each not-run check has a concrete reason.
- Subagent evidence has been reviewed and accepted, rejected, or marked stale.
- PM independent verification is recorded.
- Residual risks and rollback notes are documented.
- `FINAL_GOAL_AUDIT.md` decision is `complete`, `blocked`, or `continue`.

## Non-Completion Signals

These do not complete the PM goal by themselves:

- subagent completion;
- worker or reviewer reports;
- passing checks;
- PASS labels;
- MCP results;
- citations;
- final prose without audit evidence.
