# Goal: Workstation Workflow Compact Control Plane

## Mission

Compare the current `.codex` workspace and app-control state with the uploaded
workflow optimization documents, then keep Codex Desktop active, PM-led,
evidence-first, and subagent-capable through a compact root contract plus a
directly usable detailed runbook.

## Included

- `%USERPROFILE%\.codex\AGENTS.md`
- `%USERPROFILE%\.codex\maintenance\CODEX_DESKTOP_COMPACT_WORKFLOW.md`
- `%USERPROFILE%\.codex\config.toml` settings relevant to features, agents,
  hooks, Memento, and persistent reasoning effort
- `%USERPROFILE%\.codex\skills\SKILL_INDEX.md` posture as a harness-managed
  compact subset, not full discovery authority
- retired active roast skill packages integrated into the compact workflow
  runbook
- `clean-all-slop` separation
- managed final-handoff and sanitized config-example templates

## Excluded

- secrets, auth files, browser state, SQLite raw contents, and raw logs
- broad cleanup, deletion, package installs, MCP re-registration, or shell/PATH
  mutation unless separately requested
- product repositories outside `%USERPROFILE%\.codex`
- external publish or Git history rewrite

## Acceptance Criteria

- Root `AGENTS.md` is compact and points to the detailed compact runbook.
- The runbook records active settings, workflow, subagent policy, roast
  integration, clean-all-slop separation, verification, handoff, and rollback.
- Active settings align with the target posture or drift is recorded.
- Roast-related active `SKILL.md` packages are retired from the active skill
  root; workstation roast review routes through the compact workflow runbook and
  `workstation-workflow-full-review` source. `clean-all-slop` remains separate.
- `skills/SKILL_INDEX.md` is not manually expanded beyond its harness-managed
  template; full skill inventory is checked from directories when needed.
- Restart-regenerated forbidden transient roots and stale plugin backups are
  absent from active `.codex` state after reversible archive plus Recycle Bin
  cleanup.
- Checks are run for every changed surface where practical.
- Final handoff reports accepted and suspect subagent evidence, checks not run,
  residual risks, rollback, and status.

## Stop Condition

Stop when the current compact-workflow slice has direct verification and any
remaining findings are marked accepted, rejected, deferred, or needs-evidence.
